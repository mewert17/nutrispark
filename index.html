About

Welcome to NutriSpark, a project developed to simplify the process of tracking nutritional data. This user-friendly service is designed for anyone interested in understanding more about what nutrients they consume on a daily basis.

NutriSpark allows users to input any food item and retrieve the nutritional information from the RapidAPI Nutrition API. The nutrition facts are presented in a table format, which breaks down various macros such as calories, fats, proteins, and carbohydrates.

Additionally, the "About" page on NutriSpark dives into the functionalities of the service, explaining the technology behind it, its features, and its objectives. This page aims to educate people on how NutriSpark can be a helpful tool in building a healthier diet.

Whether your goal is to track ou macro intake, plan nutritional meals, or gain knowledge of nutritional food contents, NutriSpark is designed to support you.
